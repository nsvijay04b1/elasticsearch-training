2.4 High Availability & Fault Tolerance


Replica Recovery Process

        Primary Shard on Node A fails
                |
                v
        Replica on Node B promoted
                |
                v
        New replica allocated elsewhere




Split Brain Prevention & Quorum Logic

With 3 master nodes, quorum = 2. Majority is required for decisions.

        3 Master Nodes
        Majority Needed = 2
        Prevents dual-master scenario




Cluster Health States


•   Green – All shards assigned

•   Yellow – Replica missing

•   Red – Primary missing



2.5 Rolling Upgrade Strategy

        Step 1: Disable shard allocation
        Step 2: Stop one node
        Step 3: Upgrade version
        Step 4: Start node
        Step 5: Re-enable allocation
        Repeat per node



Ensures zero downtime upgrade in production environments.
